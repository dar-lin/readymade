Fervor

by Kristina de Coninck

Who are you?
An artist

Where do you live?
I live in France near the forest of Fontainebleau

 What is your current state of mind?
Working

 How do you define art?
Courage, patience and emotion

 What is your favorite quality in a person?
Goodness and affection

 What is your favorite quality in a piece of art?
Fervour

 Who is your favorite artist?
Albrecht Dürer

What is your favorite adjective?
Compelling

 What is your best quality?
Enthusiasm

 What is your worst habit?
My unique and daily hand rolled cigarette at 6 pm

 What is your favorite medium?
Wool and textile

 What is your favorite meal?
Japanese udon noodles

 What are you looking forward to?
Having a Belgian beer and let my mind travel while thinking of my friends
