There is an intense energy in the universe these days, based on the experiences we are going through. You can see in the pictures that the hair gravitates into different directions. Because of the stillness we are experiencing, we go back to our roots, reflecting different eras--like the Renaissance-- and appreciating the beauty of simplicity. Where are you quarantined? How did you end up there?
I am quarantined in my apartment in New York City. The day the travel ban was announced, I was shooting in Europe. The next day, I flew back to New York. I got on the last flight that was allowed to fly into New York.

Who are you quarantined with? How is it going?
I am quarantined with my wife. It is an interesting time; we recently haven’t spent this much time together because we both travel often for work. In general, I think that time apart keeps relationships fresh since you always miss each other. Luckily, we’ve been having a really good time together during this and we enjoy the solitude.

What is your current state of mind?
I’m excited and relieved since I’ve heard that non-essential workers will be allowed to go back to work soon.
