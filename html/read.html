In times of turmoil
it is hard to imagine
being creative.

But as the world seems intent
on stripping away
everything that is known,
creating feels radical, healing and essential.

We cannot govern the
chaos of our surroundings
but we can reflect
the realities of our experiences
and the inner-workings
of our deepest selves.

Right now,
we are presented with
a rare opportunity;
Why not create for
creation's sake?

Let's make art-
wherever you are,
with whatever you have.

Get READY.
